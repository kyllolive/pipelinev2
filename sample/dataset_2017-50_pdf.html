RESOLUTION NO. 14-0850-2017

After due deliberation of the SangguniangPanlungsod of the City of Lapu-Lapu, on motion of Hon. Gregorio A. Paquibot, Jr. duly seconded by Hon. Roque P. Wagwag and Hon. Elias L. Berdin,

RESOLVED, to enact, as it is hereby ENACTED the following:

ORDINANCE NO. 14-050-2017

AN ORDINANCE CREATING THE LAPU-LAPU CITY ADMINISTRATIVE BOARD

WHEREAS, the illegal drugs trade poses a real threat to peace and order and public safety in the City;

WHEREAS, there is a need to curb illegal drug activities to effectively address criminality and improve the overall peace and order situation in the city;

WHEREAS, the drive against illegal drugs by the National Government should be complemented by the city government in order to efficiently reduce supply and deter demand on the consumption of illegal drugs in the city;

WHEREAS, numerous persons arrested for illegal drug violations in the city have been found to be in the recurring places;

WHEREAS, Section 52 of the Comprehensive Dangerous Drugs Act 2002 provides for the creation of a local administrative board to abate such incidences;

WHEREAS, Joint Resolution No. 2017-02 of the City Peace and Order Council (CPOC) and the City Anti-Drug Abuse Council (CADAC) was passed requesting the Sangguniang Panlungsod to enact an ordinance creating a City Administrative Board.
