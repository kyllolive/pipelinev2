REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES CITY OF LAPULAPU OFFICE OF THE MUNICIPAL BOARD

APPROVED ORDINANCE

ORDINANCE No. 26

AN ORDINANCE NAMING AND CHANGING NAMES OF CITY STREETS WITHIN THE CITY OF LAPULAPU

Be it ordained by the Municipal Board, City of Lapulapu, Philippines, in regular session assembled, That:

SECTION 1.- The street known as Lapulapu Street, shall be changed to B.M. Dimataga Street.

SEC. 2. The street known as Washington Street, shall be changed and henceforth called S. OSMEÑA Street.

SEC. 3. - The street known as Manalili Street, shall be changed and henceforth called C. dela Berna Street.

SEC. 4. - The street known as Hamabar Street, shall be changed and henceforth called Rito dela Berna Street.

SEC. 5. - The street known as Washington St. in Marigondon, City of Lapulapu, from Marigondon Crossing and running to end up Marigondon Beach, shall be called Simeon Amoloco Street.

SEC. 6. - The street branching from Santa Cruz Street, Marigondon, Lapulapu City, Subasbasas, shall be called and henceforth known as Guarillero Street.

SEC. 7. - The street branching from the Highway at Sitio Babac running to Pag-antahan shall be called and henceforth known as B. Benghaoy Street.

SEC. 8. - The street running circumferentially from Subapenas to Subasbasas, shall be called and henceforth known as FernandoNuñez St.

SEC. 9. - The street running to Sudtunggan, Babak, Lapulapu City, from the old market, of Babak shall be called and henceforth known as Cayetano Aragano Street.

SEC. 10. - The street branching from Sudtunggan Street and running to Subapenas through Suba-masalog, shall be called and henceforth known as Agaton Sorolio Street.

SEC. 11. - The street running to the Valencia Fish Pond from S. Osmena Street, shall be known Jorge Tampus Street.

SEC. 12. - The street running to karahay from Osmena St, shall be known and henceforth called Agustin Tumulak Street.

SEC. 13. - The street branching from karahay Street and running to the direction of Kinalupanan, Loo, shall be known and henceforth called G. del Pilar Street.

SEC. 14. - The street from Mantan Rest House to Pta. Ragaño shall be known and called as Cabasa-Tasio Street.

Naming & changing names of Sta. Cruz:

SEC. 15. - The street of Bankal shall be called Mateo Pulvera Street. SEC. 16. - This ordinance shall take effect fifteen days from approval. ENACTED, June 17, 1962. APPROVED.
